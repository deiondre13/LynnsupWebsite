About Lynnsup

Lynnsup is a registered South African company (Registration No: 2019/463569/07) that provides a broad range of technical, sourcing, and industrial services.

The company is fully compliant with CIPC and committed to transparency, sustainable growth, and creating new opportunities across various sectors.

Our Vision

To be the best service provider while increasing employment opportunities for the most destitute individuals.

Our Mission

To develop a supply hub of services that enables companies to focus on their core business operations.

Lynnsup strictly adheres to all occupational health, safety, and hygiene procedures, following all regulations relevant to its operating environments.
